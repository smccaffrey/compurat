Intro

My name is Sam. I'm currently based out of Phoenix but I was borned and raised in Pittsburgh. I have interests/hobbies that include but aren't limited to movies, motorcycles, math, science, physics, data, and programming. Check out some of my awesome work.

The best way I could describe myself would be that I am very passionate about solving problems. I know this is a vague, albeit an overused statement, but it's something I stand behind. Regardless of company, industry, or field, I want to help you solve your problems. Whether it's automating your social media, helping you better manage your data, statistical analysis, modeling, or full-stack development, there is no problem that scares me.
